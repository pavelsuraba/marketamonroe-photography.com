Work

Couples

I love celebrating love! Couple shoots can be done at anytime at any age. From couples in their 20's to couples in their 80's. A couple session takes place outdoors in a field, in the city or at your favorite location. In the end, it's a storytelling session that includes the warmth you want to remember from your relationship, regardless of how young or old your love is.

Family

I love creating family portraits! They aren't just pictures, they are memories - so easily forgotten! A nice family portrait should not be missing on your walls. Take an advantage of the beautiful German landscape to share and remember your time here in Germany.

Maternity

Carrying new life under your heart is a miracle and a great blessing. There is nothing more beautiful than a glowing mother and that growing bump needs to be captured too. The best time for maternity shoot is between 7-8 months pregnant. So you are nice and rounded, but not so large that you are uncomfortable walking, sitting or standing for different shots.

Newborns

Newborn sessions are best if done within the first 14 days of birth. The best time to contact me is before you have your baby to ensure availability. I will reserve time for you around your expected due date. When the baby makes an appearance, I ask you to text, email, or call me and we can quickly schedule a day within the following two weeks.

Children

It's so easy to forget these special moments- the first steps, the first birthday, loosing their teeth, riding a bike for the first time. They've also changed so much in terms of looks. It's fun to capture how cute they are when they are young!

Wardrobe

Out are the days of wearing white and jeans or matching polos. Instead pick a nice color scheme that compliment each other, but it's not too "matchy-matchy". Don't be afraid of colors or patterns. Pick a base color, like cream, and then accent with it 1 or 2 pop colors, like navy and brown.

Bring your own personality or accessories such as scarves, hats, flowers in the hair, jewelry, bow ties, ties, sweaters, vests, jackets etc. Don't let the accessories overwhelm the subject or the photos though. Remember that the accessories should just compliment you, not be the center of the attention.

Say NO to cartoon characters and big logos as they may be distracting and take away from the subject. Shoes matter! Please don't wear sneakers or running shoes of any brand, unless we are talking about some funky Converse that go with the feel of the session...Trust me your New Balance sneakers just won't look good on your photos.
